About FreshBite

FreshBite is an online food ordering platform that delivers delicious, fresh and hygienic food from the best restaurants directly to your doorstep. Our goal is to provide fast delivery, excellent service and a smooth ordering experience.

📖 Our Story

FreshBite was created with a simple mission — to make food ordering quick, convenient and enjoyable. We connect customers with trusted restaurants while ensuring every order reaches safely and on time.
